## Zooms

    1	zoom 1:1
    2	zoom 1:2
    3	zoom to selection
    4	zoom to drawing
    5	zoom to page

Zoom history:

    `	(back quote) previous zoom
    Shift+`	next zoom

(it is stacked!)

## Tools

### Calligraphy

can be choosen by pressing `c`

## Colors

### Color bar

Cross  at the left end of the __color bar__ means "make invisible"

Click on a color to set fill, `shift`-click to set stroke
